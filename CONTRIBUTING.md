# Contributing

Contributions must improve the reference system's correctness, clarity, or evidenced engineering value.

## Before implementation

Open an issue before significant work. Describe the commerce problem, proposed scope, affected modules, alternatives, consistency implications, and validation plan. Wait for maintainer agreement on the problem and approach. Small typo or documentation corrections may proceed directly when clearly safe.

Do not add a framework, service, broker, pattern, or abstraction primarily to showcase technology or increase architectural complexity.

## Pull requests

Keep changes focused and link the accepted issue. Explain changed contracts, data ownership, failure behavior, compatibility impact, tests, and operational consequences. Use meaningful commits and follow the repository's established style.

Breaking changes require migration and rollback notes. Update relevant documentation with the implementation.

Maintainers may close unclear, inactive, duplicate, out-of-scope, low-quality, or misaligned work. Maintainers retain final authority over scope, direction, review, and acceptance while applying standards respectfully and consistently.

All participants must follow [CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md).
